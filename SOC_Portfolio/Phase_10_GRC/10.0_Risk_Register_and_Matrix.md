# Enterprise Risk Register & Risk Matrix

**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** June 2026
**Role:** GRC Analyst / Risk Manager
**Framework:** ISO 31000 (Risk Management)

---

## 1. What is a Risk Register?

A Risk Register is a centralized document that records all identified risks to the organization, their likelihood, their impact, the current controls in place to mitigate them, and the residual risk after those controls are applied. It is a living document reviewed quarterly by management.

---

## 2. Risk Scoring Methodology

We use a **5×5 Risk Matrix** based on Likelihood and Impact scales.

### 2.1 Likelihood Scale

| Score | Rating | Description |
| :---: | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | Rare | May occur only in exceptional circumstances (<5% per year) |
| 2 | Unlikely | Could occur at some time (5–25% per year) |
| 3 | Possible | Might occur (25–50% per year) |
| 4 | Likely | Will probably occur in most circumstances (50–80% per year) |
| 5 | Almost Certain | Expected to occur regularly (>80% per year) |

### 2.2 Impact Scale

| Score | Rating | Description |
| :---: | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | Negligible | Minor inconvenience, no data loss, no financial impact |
| 2 | Minor | Brief disruption (<4 hours), minor data exposure, <$10K impact |
| 3 | Moderate | Extended disruption (4–24 hours), partial data breach, $10K–$100K |
| 4 | Major | Significant disruption (1–7 days), full data breach, $100K–$1M |
| 5 | Catastrophic | Complete business halt (>7 days), regulatory penalties, >$1M |

### 2.3 Risk Matrix

|  | **Impact 1** | **Impact 2** | **Impact 3** | **Impact 4** | **Impact 5** |
| :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **Likelihood 5** | 5 (Med) | 10 (High) | 15 (High) | 20 (Crit) | 25 (Crit) |
| **Likelihood 4** | 4 (Low) | 8 (Med) | 12 (High) | 16 (High) | 20 (Crit) |
| **Likelihood 3** | 3 (Low) | 6 (Med) | 9 (Med) | 12 (High) | 15 (High) |
| **Likelihood 2** | 2 (Low) | 4 (Low) | 6 (Med) | 8 (Med) | 10 (High) |
| **Likelihood 1** | 1 (Low) | 2 (Low) | 3 (Low) | 4 (Low) | 5 (Med) |

**Risk Appetite:** The organization accepts risks scored **Low (1–4)**. Risks scored **Medium (5–9)** require a mitigation plan. Risks scored **High (10–16)** require immediate action. Risks scored **Critical (20–25)** are escalated to the CISO and Board.
